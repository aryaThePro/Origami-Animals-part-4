Origami:


Origami is the art of paper folding, which is often associated with Japanese culture. In modern usage, the word "origami" is used as an inclusive term for all folding practices, regardless of their culture of origin.

Chameleon


Interesting Facts about Chameleon


  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most Chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Pigeon


Interesting Facts about Pigeon


  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • They are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • They have excellent hearing abilities

Cicada


Interesting Facts about Cicada


  • Cicadas are known for their buzzing and clicking noises
  • Male cicadas produce this species-specific noise with vibrating membranes on their abdomens
  • The sounds vary widely, and some species are more musical than the others
